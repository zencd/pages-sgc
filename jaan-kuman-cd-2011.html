Recorded 1971-1982, released 2011
My recent purchase - a new CD-compilation of maestro Jaan Kuman. There is a lot of dope stuff including those from the rare Uno Naissoo LP, Tantsurütme EP's and some unreleased tracks also. All from the 70's.
The CD is self-released by Jaan Kuman himself and is available at Discogs.
