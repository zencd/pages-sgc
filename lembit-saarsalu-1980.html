A worth estonian jazz LP with two heavy groovers in it and that lovely naturally overdriven bass sound. There is no even a Discogs entry for that (upd: appeared). Lembit Saarsalu used to play tenor sax in the Jaan Kuman's ensemble and with the Collage band earlier, both are great collectives.
