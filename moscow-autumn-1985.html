A jazz comp from a fest called "Moscow Autumn". The interesting tracks are A2, A3 and B1 performed by Melodiya ensemble, Igor Brill's ensemble and Arsenal respectively. The soviet dixieland lobby was especially strong that year so they had pushed whole three their tracks into this compilation.
