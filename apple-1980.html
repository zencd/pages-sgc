Melodiya 4ch. LP 1980 KA90-14435-6
Apple / Яблоко is a country-folk-rock group as it is described on the LP cover. They played some progressive beats also mixed up with Russian traditional music. Seems it's the first and only quadraphonic record produced in USSR.
