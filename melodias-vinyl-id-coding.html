Melodia's vinyl ID coding

  • info
A description how soviet record label "Melodia" marked their releases.
Stereo/mono (cyrillic letters):
С — stereo
М — mono
СМ — stereo compatible with mono players
А — made from a digital master
К - quadro channel record
Г — flexible disk
Genre:
0 — hymnes, documental, political recordings
1 — symphonic, opera, choirs
2 — russian folk music
3 — non-russian folk music, but USSR's still
4 — poetry, novel, drama
5 — for children
6 — estrada, songs by soviet composers, jazz, operetta
7 — educational
8 — non-USSR folk music
9 — other
Physical size:
0 — 300 mm (giant) ≈12”
1 — 250 mm (grand) ≈10”
2 — 175 mm (mignon) ≈7”
