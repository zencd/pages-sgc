Release info:
Red Square Groove - Rare Jazz / Fusion Gems From Russian Vaults
discogs.com/release/473168
A compilation released 2000 by Cosmic Sounds Records (famous for releasing Janko Nilovic's records). This is obscured jazz (even in ex-USSR) with some groove in it. An interesting fact is every artist in this VA came from different national republics of USSR, not from the central Russia.
Tracklisting:
1 Egil Straume Jazz Combo - Ekstraversija
2 Boomerang - Dervish
3 Dustar - Dance Of The Bees
4 Boomerang - Ornament
5 Orlan - Bashkir Village's Blues
6 Alexander Rjabov & Synthesys - Expectation
7 Marina Granovskaja - Perpetum Mobile
