`Arai` is a band from Kazakhstan. This is the first album where they are backing the famous Soviet-Kazakh vocalist `Rosa Rymbaeva`. After another album they will split with Roza and start a "solo" career under a different name "A-Studio" and becoming very popular in ex-USSR.
It was tricky to get this vinyl cheap, because the release is not as worth as sellers ask for it. A1 is a funny exotic thing, B4 is a good soulful pop, the rest is just ok. A2 covers ABBA "The winner takes it all" but who cares.
