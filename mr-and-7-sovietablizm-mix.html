Ещё один годный микс от мистера And-7. Самая интересная композиция здесь на мой вкус - инструментальная обработка песни "Полюшко-поле"; не знаю на кого и подумать - на грузин разве что, но это не "Диэло" в активе которых есть своя (другая) обработка 1971 г.
Another good work by Mr. And-7 aka Jay D - a notable soviet vinyl collector and a skilled DJ also that may be noticed from this record.
I'm glad to present you a mix of Soviet jazz-funk music from USSR. I used more than 25 tracks from a variety of Soviet records of the early 70's and late 80's. Who are versed in soviet jazz and groove find for themselves as well-known and very rare tracks. I tried to make a mix that is designed primarily for chillin'... When recording a mix, I used two turntables Technics 1210mk2, mixer Rane TTM57 and Pioneer EFX-1000. No CDs, no MP3, no SERATO. Only original vinyl! Enjoy it.
